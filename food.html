Famous Food Places In AHMEDABAD

Savor the Flavor: Where Every Bite Tells a Delicious Story!

The city's food scene is a delightful tapestry of flavors, aromas, and traditions that cater to every palate. From street-side stalls to upscale restaurants, Ahmedabad offers a gastronomic journey that encapsulates the essence of its history and people.


One of the standout features of Ahmedabad's food culture is its emphasis on vegetarianism, rooted in the cultural and religious beliefs of the region. Street food is a true highlight here, with bustling markets like Manek Chowk offering a kaleidoscope of delectable options. Savor the iconic Gujarati snacks like dhokla, khandvi, and fafda, which showcase the state's love for steamed, fermented, and deep-fried delights.


Ahmedabad's culinary landscape is also marked by its embrace of modernity and innovation, while still holding onto its traditional roots. The city boasts a thriving café culture, with trendy establishments offering a fusion of global and local flavors.


Ahmedabad's food scene is a captivating blend of history, tradition, and contemporary innovation. From the bustling street food markets that awaken the senses to the sophisticated dining establishments that redefine gourmet experiences.

About Patang hotel Ahmedabad

Patang Hotel, officially Neelkanth Patang - The Revolving Restaurant is a revolving restaurant located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Built in 1980-1984, 221 feet (67 m) above the ground, it is considered as a landmark of Ahmedabad.The 110-seater Patang, set up in 1984 around Rs 1.25 crore, towers not just over Amdavad's skyline at 221 feet but is Gujarat's tallest standing structure. It offers diners a mind-blowing 360 degree view of the city in 90 minutes from its lofty perch.
