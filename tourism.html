Tourist Places In AHMEDABAD

Explore. Discover. Unfold the World.

Ahmedabad, a vibrant city in Gujarat, India, boasts a rich cultural heritage and a blend of modern and historical attractions that make it a captivating destination for tourists. One of its iconic landmarks is the Sabarmati Ashram, where Mahatma Gandhi resided during India's struggle for independence.


The city's rich heritage is reflected in its numerous pols, traditional housing clusters with intricate wooden facades. These pols offer an authentic view of Ahmedabad's architectural history. The Calico Museum of Textiles, renowned globally for its collection of historic and artistic textiles, is another gem that highlights the city's creative heritage.


In addition to historical sites, Ahmedabad embraces modernity with sites like the Science City, a dynamic space for interactive learning and exploration, and the Sarkhej Roza complex, an architectural marvel blending Islamic, Hindu, and Jain influences.


With its harmonious blend of historical charm and contemporary vigor, Ahmedabad stands as a multifaceted tourist destination that appeals to travelers with diverse interests.

About Science City

Ahmedabad's Science City is an innovative and interactive center that offers a captivating fusion of education and entertainment. This sprawling complex features a range of attractions, from its awe-inspiring IMAX theater to its engaging Hall of Space, which delves into the mysteries of the cosmos. With its interactive science exhibits, thrilling energy park, and an array of educational pavilions exploring topics like robotics, life sciences, and more, Science City provides visitors of all ages with an immersive and enlightening experience, making it a dynamic hub for learning and exploration.



Hutheesing Jain Temple:

Address:

Hutheesing Jain Temple, Camp Rd., Bardolpura, Madhupura, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380004


About Hutheesing Jain Temple

Another most beautiful and prominent religious place in Ahmedabad, Hutheesing Jain Temple was built by Seth Hutheesingh 1850 AD. Dedicated to 15th Jain Trithanakara Dharmnath the double-storied white marble building the temple is truly a must see monument. The temple is surrounded by 52 shrines each shrine decorated with an image of Tirthankara.

About Sabarmati Riverfront

The Sabarmati Riverfront in Ahmedabad is a transformative urban development project that has revitalized the city's waterfront. Once a neglected area, it has been ingeniously transformed into a picturesque space featuring beautifully landscaped gardens, recreational promenades, and vibrant cultural zones. The riverfront's aesthetic appeal, coupled with its recreational facilities like cycling tracks, parks, and public spaces, has not only provided the residents of Ahmedabad with a serene escape but has also redefined the city's identity, fostering community engagement and promoting sustainable urban living.

Adalaj Step-well:



Address:

Adalaj Rd, Adalaj, Gujarat 382421



About Adalaj Step-well

The Adalaj Step-well in Ahmedabad is a remarkable architectural marvel that stands as a testament to both functional ingenuity and artistic craftsmanship. Dating back to the 15th century, this intricately designed step-well, also known as 'vav' in Gujarati, served as a crucial water source and a cooling refuge for travelers during the region's arid climate. Adalaj Step-well's combination of utilitarian purpose and artistic expression continues to captivate visitors, making it a cherished cultural landmark in Ahmedabad.

Address:

Kankaria, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380002
Website: http://www.kankarialakefront.in/index.aspx


About Kankaria Lake

Kankaria Lake, nestled at the heart of Ahmedabad, is a serene and picturesque oasis that offers a diverse range of recreational experiences. Built in the 15th century, this circular lake has evolved into a vibrant hub for leisure and entertainment. Its well-maintained promenades, lush gardens, and family-friendly attractions, including a zoo, toy train, and amusement park, create a dynamic environment for locals and tourists alike. The lake's shimmering waters and bustling promenade come alive with people enjoying boat rides, evening strolls, and a variety of cultural events, making Kankaria Lake a cherished destination that seamlessly blends natural beauty with engaging activities.
